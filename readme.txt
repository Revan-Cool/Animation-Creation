Create your own animations colorfully! It's simple: just click and drag the mouse to draw. When the first frame is done, click the green arrow to move on to the second frame, and so forth. When your animation is complete, click the check mark, select a frame rate, and you're done! You can even save your creation by remixing. Enjoy!

• Click the red arrow to undo.
• Click the red X button to erase your entire frame.
• Click the color palette to change colors (including white for the eraser).
• Use up and down arrow keys to change brush size.
• Press the "C" key to copy the background frame.
• Hold down the "L" key to draw a straight line.
• Press the spacebar to show/hide the menu.
• Press "1" to toggle between showing the previous frame and the first frame.
